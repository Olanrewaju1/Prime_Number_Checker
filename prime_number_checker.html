Prime Number

What is a prime number?

A prime number is a whole number greater than 1 with only two factors- themselves and 1 .It can also be define as a natural number greater than 1 that is not a product of two smaller natural number. Let consider 11 as an example in which it only divisor are 1 and 11 i.e dividing 11 result in a remainder e.g 11 ÷ 6 = 1 remainder 5
NOTE: 0 and 1 are not prime number
BONUS: Any number that is not a prime number is called composite number
